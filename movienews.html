Ben Affleck No longer Directing The Batman

by Dominic Robinson

Posted February 2nd 2017 13:30 PM

The news that Ben Affleck would be directing the solo batman movie excited fans so to hear that he has dropped out of directing, has rightly made those same fans nervous for the future of the film.
Other movies like the flash have had directors sign on and leave more than once.
But after a few weeks it seems that the problem has been sorted out.
Warner Bros have found another director to helm the Movie in Matt Reeves(Director of Both Dawn and War for the Planet of the Apes.)
