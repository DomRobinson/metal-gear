Evil Dead 2

Evil Dead 2

'Groovy' : Ash Williams

A movie that serves as both a sequel to the evil dead released 6 years earlier it can also be considered a remake of it as well. Ash williams played by bruce campbell is trapped in a cabin in the woods and is constantly attacked and pestered by the evil dead. Directed once again by Sam raimi he delivers another great horror perhaps even better than the original.

9 / 10

Constantine

Constantine

' I walk my path alone because, let's be honest... Who would be crazy enough to walk it with me?' : John Constantine

Matt Ryan is John constantine. the tv series is based on dc's hellblazer comic, I havent read any of these comics but this show makes me want to. The character of constantine is played perfectly, he is interesting, has a lot of funny lines while also at the same time being dark and haunting. set in the same universe as other shows like arrow and flash it isn't afraid to go a little darker. unfortunately the show was cancelled half way through its first series.

8 / 10

Psycho

Psycho

'We all go a little mad sometimes' : Norman Bates

A horror classic directed by alfred hitchcock. Psycho has it all , memorable characters , interesting dialogue, iconic moments, suspense and a twist ending. Overall it was ahead of its time and has influenced and been imitated again and again over the past 57 years.

9 / 10
