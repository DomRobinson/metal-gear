Rogue One: A Star Wars Story

Rogue One: A Star Wars Story

'I Find That Answer Vague And Unconvincing' : K2SO

I WANTED TO LIKE THIS MOVIE but Without any attempt to make you really care about any of the characters. The only characters I ended up caring about was the droid K-2SO and Chirrut. The cast includes competent Actors that I like but ultimately did not care for their characters. I guess my biggest critique is why hire a director who has given meh offerings in the past?

8/10

Drive


Drive

'I'll see you in five minutes' : Driver

Hands down the coolest film of 2011, Drive is a stylised genre film. What it had going for it was an uncanny and moving relationship between Gosling and Mulligan. Do not go into this movie hoping that it is just a movie about fast cars because it succeeds in being more than your regualr action movie by also being a drama aswell.

9/10

Dawn of the Planet of the Apes

Dawn of the Planet of the Apes

'I always think... ape better than human. I see now... how much like them we are.' : Caesar

Dawn of the Planet of the Apes is a text book example of how to stay true to the basics of a movie while also letting it evolve into an even better and different Movie. Andy Serkis is once again amazing as Caesar and I cant wait for the sequel in 2017 also directed by matt reeves.

9/10
