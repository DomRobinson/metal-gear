Star Wars Rebels Renewed for Series 4

by Dominic Robinson

Posted March 10th 2017 13:35 PM

As Star Wars rebels is reaching the finale of its third series in a few weeks, I had no thought that the series would wrap up and end with a duel between maul and obi wan on tatooine and indeed it wont.On the 3rd of March 2017 a 4th series was announced to continue the adventures of the ghost crew. More of series 4 will be showcased at Star wars celebration in april.
